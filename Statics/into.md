## Why Statistics is Important in Data Science

Data Science is **decision-making with data**, not just coding or ML.

Statistics is important because:

* Data is noisy and messy
* Not every change is meaningful
* Not every pattern is real

Statistics helps you:

* Decide **what is normal vs abnormal**
* Avoid reacting to random fluctuations
* Make **confidence-based decisions**, not guesses

In companies:

* **Wrong statistics = wrong business decisions**
* Right statistics = money saved, fraud prevented, customers retained

Without statistics:

> Data Science becomes guesswork with charts.
